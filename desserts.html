1. Gluten Free Tahini Chocolate Chunk Cookies
These are one of our top performing cookies on the blog, and for a good reason! They’re chewy, delicious, and loaded with chocolate. And they just so happen to be gluten free, vegan, and refined sugar free.
AND they’re made in one bowl with shelf stable ingredients: tahini, oats, maple syrup, chocolate, cinnamon, and salt. If you don’t have tahini on hand, you can sub it out for any other natural, runny nut butter!
2. Chocolate Chip Cookies
Chocolate chip cookies are the ultimate at home treat. Make our recipe for the best ever chocolate chip cookies with basic ingredients you probably have in your pantry.
3. Blueberry Muffins

There’s no better way to start the day at home than with blueberry muffins! You can make these with frozen blueberries or use up any fresh blueberries you have that might be going bad!
4. Chocolate Cake
Chocolate cures all. This chocolate cake is fudgy, decadent and SO EASY to make. Top this off with a creamy chocolate frosting the ultimate at home treat! While cake uses a little more ingredients than the other recipes on this list, they’re all pantry staples: flour, sugar, cocoa powder, oil.
5. Snickers Brownies

Do you like snickers? Do you like brownies? If the answer to either of these is yes, then you’re going to absolutely LOVE these snickers brownies. They’re pretty much the best dessert with few ingredients on this list. With four decadent layers of brownie, nougat, caramel, and chocolate, you simply must make these.
